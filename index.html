What is Promstuck?

Get ready for PROMSTUCK 2027! A formal, Homestuck themed prom event complete with music, activities, special guests, and more! Our 2027 event will be taking place on August 8th, 2027 in Morgan Hill, California. Prepare for a Midnight Crew vs Felt themed night of fun!

This is an UNOFFICIAL and FAN RUN event, hosted with permission from Homestuck Official.

Attendees must be 16 years or older.

Dress Code
Promstuck is a formal Midnight crew and Felt gang themed event, so wear something fancy! Whether you cosplay a character, make a god tier outfit for yourself, or something else entirely, we encourage you to DRESS UP! Formalwear and on-theme outfits are not a requirement, however, so dress to your own comfort! You won’t be turned away at the door for dressing casually or anything, but we encourage you to dress up just for the fun of it!

Here is a chart of the recommended, but not required, dress code:

(Promstuck Dress code slightly altered directly from the FanimeCon Black and White Ball dress code)

👔 TOPS

  • Button-up long-sleeve shirts
  • Button-up short-sleeve shirts
  • Blouses
  • Dress jackets, evening jackets, suit jackets
  • Formal vests

👖 BOTTOMS

  • Dress pants
  • Slacks
  • Khakis, chinos
  • Leggings
  • Lolita ouji-style formal shorts

👗 DRESSES/SKIRTS

  • Evening Gowns
  • Dresses/skirts that are above the knee cap in length must have an opaque (not see-through) layer underneath, such as leggings, bike shorts, opaque tights, dance/cheer shorts, etc.
  • Dresses/skirts with a sheer (see-through) layer must have an opaque (not see-through) layer underneath, such as leggings, bike shorts, opaque tights, dance/cheer shorts, etc.
  • Note regarding dresses/skirts: Please be aware of how your dress/skirt moves when spinning or turning to choose which opaque under-layer works best for you

👠 SHOES

  • Dress shoes
  • Ballroom-dance shoes
  • Dress boots
  • Flats
  • Character shoes (Musical theater shoes that have either short, wide heels or are similar to Oxford-style dance shoes)
  • Formal sandals with straps to secure the heel
  • Dance sneakers
  • Jazz shoes, ballet shoes
  • Cosplay shoes using real shoes as a base
  • Heels (please know your own limits and be safe!)

🃏 PROPS AND HORNS

  • ABSOLUTELY NO prop guns
  • Props must be reasonably sized- no larger than your own body
  • Horns have a horizontal size limit of 10 inches for each horn, measuring out from the side of your head

Face Painting

Want to come in face paint? Of course you do, it's a Homestuck fan event! We have a few strict guidelines for body paint so everyone can have a good time without making a mess:
  1. Paint must be SEALED and limited to the face and neck only, which means only gloves and/or armsocks (or bare/nothing) will be allowed on your arms. Absolutely no body paint on your hands whatsoever.
  2. All face paint will be gently smear checked at the door to prevent transferring to other people, decor, or the venue. We will not touch your face for you- we will provide tissues and ask that you smear check for us.
  3. We will provide setting spray and powder for those who fail the initial smear check.
  4. Bathrooms are easily accessible in the hallway outside of the venue for makeup resealing or reapplication- just ask the staff at the door to direct you!
  5. No full coverage black face paint for carapacian cosplays. Use your brains!
The dress code is not strict, but the face paint code will be. Let’s make sure we keep this event as fun as possible, with no messes, so that everybody can have an amazing time in their cosplays and outfits and so the venue can be cleaned up easily! Thank you!
Hope to see you there!
