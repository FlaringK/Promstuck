What is Promstuck?

Get ready for PROMSTUCK! A formal, Homestuck themed prom event complete with music, activities, special guests, and more! Taking place on August 3rd, 2025 in Morgan Hill, California, prepare for a Prospit and Derse themed night of fun!

This is an UNOFFICIAL and FAN RUN event, hosted with permission from Homestuck.

We are currently guauging intrest for the turnout during early August 2025. If you feel like you'd be able and willing to make it, let us know!

Activities

Live Music

A variety of special guests will be playing live music throughout the night, both Homestuck-related and not! Take to the dance floor or listen from the sidelines as Michael Guy Bowman, Broadwaystuck, and stacylmao perform at different points during Promstuck!

Artist Alley

Miss seeing Homestuck art at conventions? Need a new poster for your room? Look no further than the Promstuck Artist Alley! Although small, we’ll have a handful of different Homestuck artists selling their wares! Artists, stay tuned for applications to open!

Special Guests

Michael Guy Bowman

Michael Guy Bowman, better known in Homestuck circles as just Bowman, has worked on Homestuck music and projects since the very beginning and continues to be a part of the fandom to this day. Known for Homestuck tracks like Sburban Jungle and albums like Mobius Trip and Hadron Kaleido, and for being an independent musician right here in the Bay Area, Bowman has performed at both unofficial and official Homestuck events in the past and we are so happy to have him perform at Promstuck!

stacylmao

Stacy, also known as stacylmao, anticore2k, and cybergeist, is a producer and DJ who is best known as the DJ that hosts the yearly Stuck at Home Con raves. Although she usually performs in virtual venues in VRChat, this time she’s bringing the Strider vibes to the stage to end the night with a rave of our own!

Visguard

We’ll be joined by the wonderful and talented cosplayer Visguard! Elijah is known for his complex and high effort Knight of Time Dave cosplays, along with his Knight aspect series of cosplays. He will be creating very special props to be awarded in our PROM ROYALTY COSPLAY CONTEST and other events! We’re so happy to have him on the team!
